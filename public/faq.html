Choose Your Plan

Choose a plan to suit our business. our plans based on the No. of lines you want to buy. Then more users, the less you pay.

Can i cancel at any time?

Yes. ClixCRM is a pay as you go service with no long term lock-in. You can cancel, upgrade and downgrade your account at any time from your account page.

When will I get billed?

You have to pay during the registration of the application for the number of months and Users subscribed for any duration. After the that you need to again pay for continuity of the services. We email a receipt each time we bill your credit card or you do the payment through online payment.

Payment methods?

You can sign up with Visa, MasterCard or American Express or even you can pay through Internal Banking.
